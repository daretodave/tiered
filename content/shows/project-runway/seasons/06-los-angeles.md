The detour. Project Runway's only season filmed off the East Coast, and its first under a new network. The LA workroom and the LA garment district both read different on camera — the light is wrong, the texture is wrong, the cast feels half a step out of frame. The judging is intact, the format is intact, but the city is doing none of the editorial work the show usually borrows from Manhattan. Included for historical importance.
