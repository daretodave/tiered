The team-format season. Project Runway's eleventh cycle opens with an extended run of group challenges, holding the workroom in committee structure far longer than the show's occasional team episodes ever had. It is the boldest format swing of the Lifetime classic era, and it reads as one — the editing tracks alliances instead of individual benches, and the runway shows carry collective fingerprints. A divisive experiment the canon respects for its nerve, even where the structure fights the format.
