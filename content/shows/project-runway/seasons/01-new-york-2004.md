The foundational season. Twelve designers in a Parsons workroom, a host the audience is meeting for the first time, and a mentor whose now-iconic walk-throughs are still finding their rhythm. The format is small and exposed in a way later seasons can't replicate — the cast is unguarded, the judging is unhurried, and the runway shows are read with patience. tiered.tv's canon places it second because every later season is a refinement of what this one invented.
