# Editor's Canon — Bachelor in Paradise

## 1. Season 1

tag: The founding season — the debut that proved the pull-from-the-franchise premise could carry its own show.
slot_argument: Filmed in Tulum with Chris Harrison hosting, it takes Bachelor and Bachelorette castoffs and runs a compressed, faster-paced rose ceremony format — the template every later season gets checked against.
community_rank_hint: rank=1 delta=0 sentiment=hold

Season one earns the top spot by default and by argument. There's no other season yet to rank it against, but the founding run does real editorial work: it takes the pull-from-the-franchise premise — stocking a beach house with Bachelor and Bachelorette contestants who didn't find their person the first time — and proves the format can sustain a season on its own, distinct from the shows it borrows its cast from. Filmed in Tulum, Mexico, with Chris Harrison hosting the same rose ceremony structure at a compressed, faster pace, it sets the template every later season either follows or pushes against. As more seasons get seeded here, this is the debut they'll be measured by.
