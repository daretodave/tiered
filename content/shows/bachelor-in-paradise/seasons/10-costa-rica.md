Season ten ends the show's nine-season run in Sayulita and relocates to Costa Rica, the first time the format has filmed outside Mexico, while returning to its traditional summer premiere. The cast expands to include contestants from the Golden Bachelor and Golden Bachelorette spinoffs, and Hannah Brown joins Wells Adams behind the bar. Later episodes add a voting-based mechanic for deciding who distributes roses.
