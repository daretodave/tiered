Drag Race's VH1 debut, and the format's biggest production-
scale jump to date. The premiere opened with Lady Gaga at the
judge's table, a signal of the cultural altitude the show had
reached. The cast leaned charismatic and varied across drag
styles, and the workroom-and-runway shape held its weight under
the bigger network treatment. RuPaul Charles hosted alongside
Michelle Visage and a rotating fourth chair. The season reset
the franchise's reach without flattening its craft.
