The split-premiere season — two workroom intros, two cast
halves, one merge before the format settled. The lineup ran
deep on identity and craft, with queens whose individual
aesthetics were already fully formed walking in. Snatch Game
and the Shakespeare challenge produced some of the franchise's
most-replayed runway moments. RuPaul Charles hosted with
Michelle Visage and a rotating fourth chair. The season's
cultural footprint stretched well past its Logo airing into the
seasons that followed.
