A season built on combustible casting. Season 11 stacks the
workroom with large, confrontational personalities, and the edit
spends its energy on the friction rather than the sewing
machine. RuPaul Charles and Michelle Visage anchor a panel over
one of the era's most argued-over casts. The drama is the engine
here, which makes for a watchable hour but a less even contest:
the runway craft doesn't always keep pace with the noise around
it. Loud, divisive, never boring.
