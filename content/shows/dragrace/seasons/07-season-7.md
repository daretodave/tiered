The last Logo-era season, and an uneven one. Season 7 runs a
large cast with a more polished edit than the early years, but
the workroom stays cooler than the seasons around it: the
personalities don't lock together the way Season 4 or 6 managed.
RuPaul Charles and Michelle Visage anchor a familiar panel. The
runway craft holds up; the contest around it drifts. Watch it as
the closing Logo chapter before the network move reset the
scale.
