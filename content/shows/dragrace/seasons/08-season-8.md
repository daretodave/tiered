The most polished season Logo produced before handing off to
VH1. Season 8 runs a tighter, glossier cast and leans the edit
toward the runway, previewing the production scale the network
move would bring. RuPaul Charles and Michelle
Visage anchor the panel. The craft is consistently high and the
season looks expensive for the era, but the workroom runs
smoother and less combustible than the franchise's hottest
years. A handsome bridge: strong to look at, low on friction.
