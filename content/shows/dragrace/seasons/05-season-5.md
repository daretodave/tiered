Logo TV's last era of small-scale Drag Race, and the franchise
at its sharpest as a contest. The cast had depth across design,
comedy and performance, with Snatch Game landing some of the
most-cited turns the format has produced. RuPaul Charles hosted
alongside Michelle Visage, Santino Rice and a rotating fourth
chair. The workroom dynamics ran hot. The runway categories
pulled craft out of the lineup week after week without
overproducing the result.
