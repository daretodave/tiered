Summer House's fourth season is its steadiest yet: the Watermill house holds for a third consecutive year, and turnover is lighter than any season before it. Two new faces join the main cast while only one prior season's newcomer scales back to a guest role. Sixteen episodes, the show's longest order to date, let an increasingly settled ensemble run at full comfort.
