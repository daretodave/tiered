Summer House's sophomore season starts with a move: after East Hampton won't renew the house's permit, the group relocates to Watermill for the first time. Amanda Batula is elevated from a recurring friend role to the main cast, joined by two newcomers, while three of the original housemates depart for good and two more scale back to guest appearances. Twelve episodes track a familiar group finding its footing in an unfamiliar house.
