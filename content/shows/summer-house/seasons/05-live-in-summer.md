Summer House's fifth season breaks its own format: with the pandemic reshaping normal life, the cast moves in together full-time for six weeks instead of commuting out on weekends, working remotely from the same Hamptons house they'd otherwise visit for two days at a stretch. Ciara Miller joins the main cast for the shift. Fourteen episodes capture a format built on distance suddenly collapsing that distance entirely.
