# Editor's Canon — Summer House

## 1. Amagansett

Summer House's debut season sets the format with total confidence: a fixed group of New York City friends who work all week in the city and pile into one rented house in Amagansett, New York, every weekend of the summer. There's no host, no competition, and no prize — just a shared roof and a long commute doing the work of building stakes. What makes the season hold up isn't spectacle, it's how fast the ensemble reads as an actual friend group rather than a cast assembled for cameras. Nine seasons of relocation and expansion haven't replaced what this one established first, which is why it holds this canon's only ranked slot.
