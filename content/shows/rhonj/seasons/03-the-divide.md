The season that became the franchise's defining argument. New cast members arrive from within the existing family — people who share blood and history, not just a zip code — and the show's social geometry locks into its most consequential configuration. Seven people with genuine, complicated pre-existing relationships and nowhere to retreat. The north Jersey setting shifts from backdrop to pressure system. Season three is the franchise at its most distinct: conflict with real roots, stakes that don't need explaining.
