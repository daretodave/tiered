The franchise's gentlest early season, and it earns that register. Season five attempts to move the family conflict toward resolution — the cast works in good faith, the arc has a direction, and the production handles the emotional temperature shift without forcing drama the season isn't generating. The intensity of the feud era recedes, but the show stays watchable throughout. Not the franchise at its peak pressure, but an honest account of what follows when that pressure begins to ease.
