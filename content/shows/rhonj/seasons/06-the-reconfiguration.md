Dina Manzo's return offers one thread of continuity; three entirely new Housewives supply the fresh energy. The reconfigured cast hasn't settled into its own chemistry yet, and a publicly reported legal situation hangs over the season's atmosphere without the show being able to engage it directly. A transitional season that earns its place by holding the format together through a difficult pivot.
