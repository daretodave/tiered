The season that made RHONY a cultural institution. A group trip to St. John — immediately and permanently known as Scary Island — produced some of the most committed, strange, and wholly unscripted television the franchise has ever aired. The New York scenes are strong; the trip is something else entirely. Season three is what the whole format had been building toward, without knowing it.
