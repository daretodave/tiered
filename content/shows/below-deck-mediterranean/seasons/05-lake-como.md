Season 5 took Below Deck Mediterranean off open saltwater and onto Lake Como — the franchise's first freshwater season. Shorter passages meant less deck time and more port-side pressure, shifting the tension toward the interior department in ways the Adriatic seasons didn't. Northern Italy gave the production a visual register entirely its own. Captain Sandy adapted her command to the new geography without losing authority. The location felt like an editorial choice, not a stunt.
