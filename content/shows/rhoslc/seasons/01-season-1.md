Six women, one Salt Lake City social circle, and a founding cast bigger than most Housewives debuts attempt. Lisa Barlow, Mary Cosby, Heather Gay, Meredith Marks, Whitney Rose, and Jen Shah bring an LDS-adjacent social world to Bravo for the first time, and the show leans into that specificity rather than treating it as backdrop. It's a confident, well-cast debut with a social register the franchise hadn't filmed before.
