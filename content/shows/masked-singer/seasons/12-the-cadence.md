The format ran exactly as built — ambitious costumes, a celebrity roster calibrated to sustain the guessing conceit, and a panel whose dynamic had eleven prior cycles of chemistry behind it. The cadence of the schedule is the story: the show as a year-round fixture, each cycle doing its job without needing to announce itself.
