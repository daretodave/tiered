Season fourteen arrived with the established era's first real disruption: Rita Ora took the panel seat held by Nicole Scherzinger across thirteen prior cycles, breaking a run of unbroken continuity. The season leaned into theme-night density — Fear Night, Twilight Night, Spice Girls Night among them — and added a wrinkle no prior cycle carried: a contestant competing masked while also serving in a season-exclusive "Insider" role behind the scenes.
