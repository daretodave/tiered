Reaching ten seasons without a format overhaul is a statement. The Masked Singer arrived at its double-digit cycle recognizably intact — the same panel, the same disguise conceit, the same structure that worked in 2019 still working in 2023. The costume design stayed ambitious. The celebrity roster kept the calibration the format depends on. Not the show at its peak, but the show proving the original design was durable enough for a full decade.
