MasterChef: Legends runs a guest-judge format unlike any prior season. Each week a culinary legend — Emeril Lagasse, Curtis Stone, Roy Choi, Nancy Silverton, and others, including a returning Graham Elliot — joins the permanent panel. Fifteen home cooks is the smallest cast in years, keeping the challenges focused. The pandemic halted filming mid-production; the season resumed and aired in 2021. The format concept holds through the disruption.
