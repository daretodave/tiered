Gordon Ramsay, Graham Elliot, and Joe Bastianich run the first American MasterChef across thirteen tight episodes. The white-apron audition, the mystery box, the pressure test — the format is building its elimination grammar in real time, and the seams show in ways that are interesting rather than damaging. The three-judge chemistry is still forming. The cooking level is genuinely aspirational for a home-cook field. A foundational first draft with rough edges worth watching.
