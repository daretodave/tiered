Season eight ends the rotating-judge period and gives MasterChef a stable three-judge panel again — Gordon Ramsay, Aaron Sanchez, and Christina Tosi. Sanchez's permanent presence adds a Mexican and Tex-Mex perspective the format hadn't carried before. The panel is cohesive and the cooking is solid, but the season functions mostly as a transitional year: Tosi's departure after this run means the show is rebuilding its judging identity for the second time in three seasons.
