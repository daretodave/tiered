Season seven replaces the fixed third-judge seat with a revolving door. Guest chefs — Wolfgang Puck, Aaron Sanchez, and others — rotate alongside Ramsay and Tosi each week, bringing fresh perspectives but no continuity. The Skills Test arrives as a new challenge type, and a three-way finale replaces the standard head-to-head. Without a permanent third judge, the judging dynamic resets weekly. The format experimentation is genuine; the continuity cost is real.
