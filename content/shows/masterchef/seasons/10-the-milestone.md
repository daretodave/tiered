Season ten is MasterChef at its most ambitious. The milestone tenth anniversary brings the show's longest episode count, a new Battle Pass mechanic that changes how immunity and advantage are earned, and the first international field challenges in the US series — filmed at Hatfield House in England. Guest judges Grant Achatz and Masaharu Morimoto raise the culinary stakes. Ramsay, Sanchez, and Bastianich are a settled panel. The ambition is earned.
