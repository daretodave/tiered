MasterChef: Dynamic Duos is the show's most radical reinvention in a single season — new city, new judge, new format structure all at once. Twenty-four home cooks travel to Melbourne to compete as twelve pairs throughout the run, with relationships ranging from married couples to siblings to best friends shaping how the cooking decisions are made. Tiffany Derry arrives as the third permanent judge, replacing Aaron Sanchez. The pairs format produces different dramatic textures than individual play.
