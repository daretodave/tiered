— winning teams had observable reasons to win, losing teams had observable reasons to lose
— and the boardroom applied consistent logic throughout. Eighteen contestants with enough
variety that different challenges surfaced different strengths. The show had learned by this
point what it needed from a cast: people competing on the week's actual task. The founding
