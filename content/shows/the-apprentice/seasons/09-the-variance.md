reshuffles them partway through much like the season before it. What sets this run
apart is the boardroom's inconsistency — one episode dismisses three people before a
task even begins, another lets everyone stay. The unevenness reads as a format still
hunting for a stable rhythm three cycles into the celebrity pivot.
