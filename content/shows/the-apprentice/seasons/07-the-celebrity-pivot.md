The show's identity changes here. Fourteen celebrities move into the Trump International
Hotel and Tower, split by gender into Empresario and Hydra, and compete for money that
goes to charity rather than a paycheck or a job. The tasks still follow the founding
format, but the stakes shift from career to reputation, and the boardroom starts weighing
star power alongside performance. A genuine format pivot, not a retread.
