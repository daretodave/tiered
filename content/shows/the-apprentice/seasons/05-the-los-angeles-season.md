Season five relocated to Los Angeles and kept the business competition format intact.
The tasks took on an entertainment-industry character — studio proximity and the LA
market gave the creative briefs a different texture from the New York seasons — while
the core structure held: two teams, weekly tasks, boardroom accountability. The casting
delivered competitive range, and the boardroom weighed actual field performance. The
founding era's closing chapter, and a solid one.
