The New Celebrity Apprentice is a hard reset. Arnold Schwarzenegger takes over hosting
duties — the first change of host in the show's history — with new opening titles and a
redesigned boardroom signaling a clean break from everything before it. The task format
stays recognizable, but the continuity that carried through fourteen prior seasons is gone.
A new era starts from zero.
