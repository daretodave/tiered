After three celebrity-cast runs, the show returns to civilian hopefuls — sixteen
contestants selected specifically because the recession had upended their careers,
split into Fortitude and Octane with players moving between teams as the season
goes. Rewards shift from luxury prizes to meetings with real business leaders, and
a mid-season disqualification for manipulating a task result gives the boardroom
teeth again. The last civilian season the show ever ran.
