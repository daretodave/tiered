The second celebrity run brings sixteen contestants split into Athena and KOTU, but
attrition on the men's side forces the show to reshuffle its teams partway through —
a wrinkle the founding format never needed. A double-elimination episode adds a jolt
the civilian seasons rarely reached for, and the final task gets judged against five
separate criteria instead of a single boardroom call. More format engineering than
task-driven drama.
