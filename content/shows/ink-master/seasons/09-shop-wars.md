Shop Wars is the format's most structural swing yet: nine rookie shops against nine veteran shops, competing in a tag-team elimination format where the shop's fate matters more than any single artist's. The prize itself moves — for the first time, a shop wins together rather than an individual artist walking away alone. It's the clearest test the format has run of whether teamwork translates to better tattooing under real pressure.
