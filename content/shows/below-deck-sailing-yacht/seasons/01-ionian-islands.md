The Below Deck franchise moved to a sailing yacht and the Greek Ionian Islands, and the format had to rebuild itself around the new vessel's demands. Captain Glenn Shephard commanded his first charter crew through waters that rewarded seamanship over horsepower — a technical register the motor yacht show had never required. The Ionian backdrop gave the show a Mediterranean clarity from the start. Unpolished in the ways origin seasons always are, and foundational for the same reason.
