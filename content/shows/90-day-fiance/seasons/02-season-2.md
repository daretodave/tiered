Season two adds a sixth couple to the format, the first sign that 90 Day Fiancé is thinking beyond a single cast of five. Partners arrive from a wider spread of countries, and the visa clock runs on every couple at once rather than following one relationship at a time. The show is still finding its rhythm, but the six-couple structure introduced here becomes the template the franchise keeps returning to.
