The third season trades the tropical beach for a Kenyan game
reserve, and Survivor gains a survival layer it has rarely had
since. The cast deals with cold nights, scarce water, and a
camp inside an electric perimeter to keep the lions out. The
pace is slower, the misery is real, and the show lets the
landscape carry the mood. Africa is the season that proved
Survivor could change shape without losing what it was.
