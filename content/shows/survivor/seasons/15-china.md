The fifteenth season opens at the Forbidden City and moves
to a lakeside camp in Jiangxi Province, the franchise's
first network-level shoot inside mainland China. The cast is
the cleanest mid-2000s lineup the show had assembled, and
the post-merge stretch is among the strongest classic-era
runs the format produced. China is often pointed to as a
peak of pre-strategy-renaissance Survivor — a quiet, dense
season that earns its rank the simple way.
