The first modern-era Fiji season, shot on the Mamanuca
Islands the franchise would settle into long-term. The cast
splits along generational lines, and the premise plays as a
casting frame the editors lean on without overworking. The
casting bench is the deepest the show had assembled since
the strategy renaissance peaked, and the Mamanuca location
runs as a permanent base from this season forward. Jeff
Probst hosts a season the franchise quietly used as the
beginning of its modern home.
