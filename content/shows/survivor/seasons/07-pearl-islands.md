A pirate-coded season in Panama, dropped into an early run of
Survivor that was still finding what its loud setting looked
like. The cast leans into the theme — big personalities, sharp
edges, a marooning that asks them to buy their own supplies in
a Panamanian village. The Outcasts twist arrives mid-season and
bends the format in a way the show keeps flirting with for years
afterward. Pearl Islands is where Survivor learns it can swing
big.
