Twenty former champions on one beach in Fiji, framed by the show
as its season-forty milestone and shot to feel like one. Edge of
Extinction returns and the fire-token economy gives players a
currency to scheme around. Every conversation carries the weight
of two decades of history, and the cast plays accordingly — wary,
respectful, and loud when it counts. The last pre-pandemic season
the show would air, and a wide-screen retrospective of how
Survivor learned to play itself.
