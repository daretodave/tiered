Three tribes split by archetype — Brains, Brawn, Beauty — and
the conceit holds up because the casting does. Cagayan is where
modern Survivor's tactical grammar mostly gets written: idol
plays, vote splits, advantage management, the read-the-room
paranoia that drives every season since. The energy is loud and
confident, a cast that walked in knowing the game and wanted to
play it hard. Jeff Probst hosts a season that knew exactly what
it was making.
