The twelfth season returns to the Pearl Islands and installs
the two pieces of grammar the modern game runs on — Exile
Island and the hidden immunity idol. The opening splits the
cast into four small tribes that collapse to two after a vote,
a structural premise the show has never quite repeated at this
scale. Panama is a season-long pilot for what Survivor would
become from this point forward, and the cast plays the
new game with real curiosity.
