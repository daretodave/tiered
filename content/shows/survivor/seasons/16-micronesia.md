The sixteenth season lands the returnee experiment the show
had been reaching for since All-Stars. Ten favorites from
the first fourteen casts opposite ten fans who'd applied to
play, shot in the Philippines on a location production
would re-use. The post-merge stretch is the dense strategic
run every modern returnee season tries to imitate. Many
editors place Micronesia in the top five all-time, and the
canon agrees — this is what the format looks like at full
strength.
