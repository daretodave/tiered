The second Brains/Brawn/Beauty season, two years after
Cagayan wrote the template. Six players per archetype this
time, and the casting team leans into the brief — each tribe
arrives with a sharper internal shape. Koh Rong's heat
profile reads on camera in a way classic shoots rarely
allowed, and the post-merge stretch carries the strategic
vocabulary the show had spent two seasons consolidating.
Jeff Probst hosts a season the canon often cites as the
strongest twist-heavy-era return-to-form.
