The fourteenth season runs a haves-versus-have-nots premise
that asks the cast to play under unequal conditions through
the entire pre-merge. The structural experiment lands
awkwardly; the cast carries texture once the merge arrives
and the camp asymmetry stops being the story. This is the
first Survivor shoot on the Macuata coast, a location
production would return to repeatedly. Fiji is a flagging
mid-2000s season the canon has to weigh honestly.
