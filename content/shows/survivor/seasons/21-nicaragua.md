The twenty-first season pitches camp at San Juan del Sur,
the show's first Nicaraguan shoot, and runs the format's
loudest twist experiment to date. A Medallion of Power
advantage, a reworked hidden-idol clue system, and a tribal
split drawn by age all arrive in one season. The
experiments outpace the cast, and the season's fandom
reputation has stayed divided. Nicaragua is the show
pushing the format faster than the players were ready to
push back.
