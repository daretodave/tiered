The twenty-third season returns to Upolu for a second
Redemption Island run, with two more veterans — Coach and
Ozzy — anchoring a hero-versus-villain casting frame. The
duel mechanic settles into something workable, and the
season is the first time the show clearly committed to
returnees in matched pairs as a recurring casting move.
South Pacific is the format pulling Redemption Island into
a shape it could use, even where the season's loud
returnee framing stays the dominant note.
