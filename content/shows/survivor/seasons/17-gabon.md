The seventeenth season heads back to the African continent
for the show's second hard-conditions shoot, this time on
the Gabonese coast. Elephants block the water sources, the
heat is real, and the cast skews younger than usual. The
editing leans on landscape — aerials, wide tracking shots,
the texture of dry savanna — in stretches the strategy
can't quite fill. Gabon is uneven by spans, but the
cinematography earns its place in the canon's bottom
shelf.
