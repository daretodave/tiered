Six couples take on the same ultimatum a fourth time:
propose within weeks, or split up and trial-marry someone
new from inside the group for three weeks. What's new is the
setting — Las Vegas, the first city in the franchise's run
where the cast already lives. That closeness changes the
calculus: the deadline isn't just about a relationship, it's
about staying in the life these couples already built.
