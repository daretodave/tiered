Season 8 returns to a standard weekly elimination after several
cycles of experimental mechanics, running it alongside something
new: Fame Games, a parallel fan-vote side-competition. Eliminated
queens' runway looks replay during Untucked, and viewers vote
for a separate cash-prize winner apart from the main game. Ts
Madison joins the judging panel as a permanent member for the
first time. RuPaul Charles hosts twelve episodes across a
twelve-queen cast.
