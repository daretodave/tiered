All Winners gathers eight former champions and removes weekly
elimination entirely. Each week's top two performers earn
Legendary Legend Star status and lip sync each other for cash
tips, and the winner can block a rival from a future challenge.
Every queen stays in the game all season, right up to a finale
that splits into two separate title tracks — a top prize and a
smaller consolation prize. RuPaul Charles hosts twelve episodes.
