Season 9 removes weekly elimination again, this time with a
twist Season 7 didn't try: the stakes are charitable, not
personal. Each week's top two performers earn Beautiful
Benefactress Badges and lip sync for donations to The Palette
Fund, with badge winners able to block a rival from earning one
the next week. The three queens with the most badges reach the
finale lip sync. RuPaul Charles hosts twelve episodes.
