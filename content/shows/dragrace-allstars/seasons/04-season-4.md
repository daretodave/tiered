Season 4 keeps the winner-picks-eliminee model from prior
cycles, then complicates it twice: one week suspends elimination
outright, and a later LaLaParUza round lets eliminated queens
lip sync their way back into the game. The finale caps the
season with a result the franchise has never repeated before or
since. RuPaul Charles hosts ten episodes on VH1, with the full
standard judging panel back together.
