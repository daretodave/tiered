Season 6 brings the largest All Stars cast yet — thirteen
returning queens — to the format's new streaming home on
Paramount+. Lip Sync Assassin returns from the prior cycle, then
tightens partway through: only the challenge winner stays safe,
and everyone else becomes vulnerable regardless of placement. A
new side ladder, RuDemption, lets each eliminated queen lip sync
the reigning champion for a shot at re-entry. RuPaul Charles
hosts twelve episodes.
