Season 2 hands the fate of the cast to the cast itself.
Ten queens pulled from the show's second through seventh seasons
compete under a new mechanic: the top two lip sync for the win,
and whoever wins chooses which struggling queen goes home, plus
a $10,000 tip. Todrick Hall sits in for Ross Mathews on the
panel. Nine episodes, Logo TV, RuPaul Charles hosting.
