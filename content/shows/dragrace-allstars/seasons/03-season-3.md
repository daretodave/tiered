Season 3 moves the show to VH1 and keeps Lip Sync For Your
Legacy running much as Season 2 left it — the top two lip sync,
and the winner chooses who goes home. The real addition lands at
the finale: previously eliminated queens return as a jury,
voting on which two of the final four get the last lip sync for
the $100,000 prize. RuPaul Charles hosts eight episodes.
