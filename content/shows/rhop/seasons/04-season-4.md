Season four is RHOP's first season with zero full-time cast turnover — the same six housewives from Season 3 return with nobody new added and nobody gone. Katie Rost reappears briefly as a guest, but the core group carries the twenty-one episodes on established chemistry alone. It's the clearest test yet of whether the format holds up without a new face to reset the group's dynamics.
