Season seven runs with the exact same seven housewives from Season 6, the most cast-stable season RHOP had produced to that point. No new housewife arrives and nobody leaves, which means the season can lean entirely on relationships the group has already spent a full year building. Twenty episodes give a settled cast plenty of room to work without a newcomer resetting the dynamic.
