New York. Top Chef returns to the East Coast for a Manhattan-
staged season with Padma Lakshmi hosting, Tom Colicchio at
the judge's table, and Gail Simmons in the critic seat. The

brief — borough food cultures, immigrant kitchens, the weight

itself against the city with the loudest restaurant tradition
in the country.
