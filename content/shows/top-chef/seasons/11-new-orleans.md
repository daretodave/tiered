The city carried the season. Creole and Cajun traditions, gulf
seafood, brunch culture and the long shadow of New Orleans
restaurants framed the challenges from the first cook. The cast
skewed warm without losing competitive edge, the kitchen
tension stayed about the food, and Emeril Lagasse joined Tom
Colicchio and Gail Simmons at the judge's table. Padma Lakshmi
hosted a season that let its setting do real work.
