Seattle. Top Chef heads to the Pacific Northwest with Padma
Lakshmi hosting, Tom Colicchio at the judge's table, and Gail
Simmons in the critic seat. The season leans into Seattle's
ingredient access — Pacific seafood, mushroom country, the
regional pantry — and the cast argues hard against the Las
Vegas standard. The Texas-derived qualifying round opens the
season, the cooking runs at restaurant level, and the regional
fluency is the franchise's tightest in years.
