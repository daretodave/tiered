All-Stars L.A. Top Chef brings back a roster of fan-favorite
alumni — cooks who fell short the first time and came back
with something to settle. Padma Lakshmi hosts, Tom Colicchio
runs the judge's table, Gail Simmons sits in the critic seat.
The cast plays with the audience's prior knowledge of them,
the challenges run harder because everyone has done this
before, and the bench is deep enough to justify the event.
The strongest returnee season since the original All-Stars.
