Colorado. Top Chef takes the road show to altitude, running
the regional-immersive grammar across Denver, Boulder,
Telluride and Aspen. Padma Lakshmi hosts, Tom Colicchio holds
the judge's table, Gail Simmons sits in the critic seat. The
mountain setting does real work — elevation changes how food
cooks, and the briefs lean into it. The cast adapts across
shifting pantries and thin-air kitchens, and the editing
handles the geography with the franchise's mature road-show
muscle. A solid late-Padma immersive.
