Top Chef went on the road for the first time, splitting the
season across Austin, San Antonio, Dallas and Houston before
settling into a finale run. The cast doubled in the qualifying
rounds, Last Chance Kitchen debuted as a parallel mechanic, and
the challenges leaned into regional staples without flattening
them into cliche. Padma Lakshmi hosted alongside Tom Colicchio,
Hugh Acheson, Emeril Lagasse and Gail Simmons across a longer
runway than the format usually permits.
