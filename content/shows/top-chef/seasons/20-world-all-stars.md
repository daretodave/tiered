World All-Stars. Top Chef goes global, bringing alumni from
Top Chef franchises around the world to London, with the
finale running in Paris. Padma Lakshmi hosts her final
season, Tom Colicchio runs a supersized judge's table, Gail
Simmons sits in the critic seat. The cast is drawn from
kitchens the U.S. flagship had never cooked against, and the
cross-franchise premise gives the season a register it had
never used. An ambitious international swing and the close of
the Padma era.
