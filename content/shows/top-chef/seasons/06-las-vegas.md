The Strip provided the staging — Mandalay Bay kitchens,
casino-floor stunt challenges, the buffet of culinary excess
Vegas invites. The cast walked in technically ready, with the
deepest pre-show resumes the franchise had recruited at the
time. Padma Lakshmi hosted with Tom Colicchio and Gail Simmons
at the table, joined by guest judges who treated the food
seriously. The season set the bar future Top Chefs have
measured themselves against.
