hosting, Tom Colicchio at the judge's table, and Gail Simmons
in the critic seat. The season uses Boston's restaurant scene
and the wider New England pantry — Atlantic seafood, Yankee
traditions, the immigrant kitchens layered into the city — as
its working register. The cast cooks competently, the
challenges hold their shape, and the judges' regional fluency
shows. A solid late-Padma run that the surrounding seasons
