Nick Lachey returns to host, and the show moves from Panama to
Tulum, Mexico, for a season that finally earns a real structural
twist. A mixer event lets previously eliminated contestants back
into the villa to match against whoever's still standing — a
genuine escalation of the stakes, not just a bigger cast list.
That cast draws from Love Is Blind, Squid Game: The Challenge,
The Mole, and more. Ten tight episodes.
