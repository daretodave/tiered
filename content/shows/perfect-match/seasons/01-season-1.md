Contestants who fell in and out of love on other Netflix dating
shows — Love Is Blind, Too Hot to Handle, The Circle, and more —
get pulled into one house and rematched from the ground up. Nick
Lachey hosts a crossover experiment that could have played as a
cheap reunion special. Instead it treats a cast that already
knows how to perform for cameras like the real test it actually
is.
