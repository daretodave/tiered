The strangest experiment of the early canon. Thirteen houseguests, except one slot is held by a pair of identical twins who swap in and out under the same name across the opening weeks, and the rest of the house has no idea. Project DNA is a structural gamble the show would never try again at this scale, and the seams show. Worth the watch for the audacity, and for proof that Big Brother was still gambling in year five.
