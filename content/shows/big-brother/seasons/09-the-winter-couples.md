The only Big Brother season to air in winter, slotted into CBS's lineup during the 2008 writer's strike. Sixteen houseguests move in and are paired into couples on Day One — "'Til Death Do You Part" built out as a structural premise. The off-season slot shows. The casting feels rushed, the chemistry uneven, and the twist constrains the game more than it opens it. A historical curiosity more than a rewatch, the run that earned the show its summer-only future.
