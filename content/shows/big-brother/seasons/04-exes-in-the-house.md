The franchise's first big casting swing. Thirteen houseguests enter, and on Day One the show reveals the catch — every houseguest has an ex already inside. The X-Factor twist gives the season a charge the prior runs never had, and the producers seem freshly aware of what reality framing can do when the cast walks in with shared history. The pacing is uneven, the twist mechanics still rough, but the swing matters. The blueprint for every relationship-twist season to come.
