The most twist-mechanized framing the franchise has attempted.
An in-house artificial-intelligence theme controls the season's
twists, the AI Arena weekly competition decides a final nominee,
and BB Instant power drops can land without warning. Sixteen
newcomers, thirty episodes, anchored by Julie Chen Moonves. A
season that hands twist authority to a system rather than a
house, and a clear study in how far the modern format can push
its own machinery on screen.
