Twelve self-contained episodes, each built around a single construction brief: a judge from the Alone alumni pool challenges three fellow veterans to build something specific — a functional shelter, a watercraft, a bridge, a food trap — in three days, using a shared limited tool kit and whatever their local land offers. There's no wilderness drop, no elimination season arc. The appeal is the craft itself: watching experienced survivalists solve the same problem across radically different terrain.
