The season the passport reopens. After two confined pandemic-era runs, the franchise travels again — Indian Wells, the lead's home state of Minnesota, and Mexico — with the alumnae hosting duo returning for their second outing. Eleven episodes and a poised, articulate elementary-school-teacher lead who runs the cocktail parties with unusual composure. The location work feels earned rather than routine after the single-resort years. A strong fall season that quietly restored the format's range, well-built and easy to recommend.
