The franchise's second full returnee outing, four years after
All-Stars. Eleven teams from prior seasons came back to a
spring route through Australia, Japan, Switzerland,
Liechtenstein, Austria, India, and Brazil before finishing in
Miami. CBS held the Sunday hour and the editing leaned hard
into the histories the audience already had with the field.
Phil Keoghan hosts a returnee season that re-tests the
all-stars template against a different generation of teams.
