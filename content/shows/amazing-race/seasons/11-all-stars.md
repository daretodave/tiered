The first full returnee season, with eleven teams pulled
from the show's first ten years back on the starting line.
The route ran through Central and South America, southern
Europe and Asia, and the cast brought the kind of
confrontational chemistry only history between players can
produce. Phil Keoghan hosts a field that already knows the
rhythms of the format. Every later returnee event in the
race canon answers to the template set here.
