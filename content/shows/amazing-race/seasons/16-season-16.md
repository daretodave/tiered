Eleven teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg spring route
through Chile, Argentina, Germany, France, the Seychelles,
Malaysia, Singapore, and China. CBS held the Sunday hour and
the production ran the format on muscle memory — the kind of
season the franchise can build in its sleep after a full
decade on the road. The casting leans on the dating-couple
and partner-pair archetypes the show had refined. Phil
Keoghan keeps the pit-stop cadence honest across the legs.
