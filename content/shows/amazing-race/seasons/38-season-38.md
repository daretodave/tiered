A historic franchise crossover. Thirteen teams of two, each
built around at least one former US Big Brother houseguest, left
Hoorn, the Netherlands, the birthplace of the Big Brother
format, into a 7,500-mile route through the Czech Republic,
Hungary, Croatia, Romania, Greece, Italy, and France before a
final leg to New York City. The toolkit returns the Express
Pass, Hazard, and a pre-starting-line task, and adds a U-Turn
penalty for a non-elimination survivor, the season's one
genuinely new mechanic.
