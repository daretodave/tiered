Eleven teams left Santa Barbara into a twelve-leg spring
route through Argentina, Paraguay, Italy, Germany, Tanzania,
India, Azerbaijan, and Turkey before a Hawaiian finish line.
CBS held the Sunday hour and the production ran the format
in its established lane — the version the show had been
making for a decade now. The casting leans on partner pairs
and dating couples. Phil Keoghan paces an outing that is
competent across the legs without reaching for novelty.
