Eleven teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg autumn route
through Taiwan, Indonesia, Malawi, Denmark, Belgium, the
Netherlands, and Panama before finishing in Atlanta. CBS held
the Sunday hour and the production ran the format with the
muscle memory of eleven years on the road. The casting
widens across age and partner type. Phil Keoghan hosts a
follow-up to the returnee spring outing whose cast couldn't
quite carry the editing room into the same kind of season.
