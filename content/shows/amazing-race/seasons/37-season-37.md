Branded a season of surprises. Fourteen teams, the largest US
cast the franchise has ever run, left Los Angeles through Hong
Kong, Japan, Bulgaria, France, Portugal, and Dubai before a
Miami finish, with several first-time stops along the way. The
premise is that every leg carries a twist: Fork in the Road,
Driver's Seat, and Valet Roulette debut alongside returning
mechanics like the Intersection and Head-to-Head. The widest
field the show has ever sent, kept off balance by design.
