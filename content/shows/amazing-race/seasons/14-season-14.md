Eleven teams started from Locarno into a twelve-leg route
through Romania, Russia, India, Thailand, and China. CBS held
the Sunday hour and the production ran the format cleanly
inside the rhythms it had spent eight years tuning. The
casting leans on long-running partner duos and the editing
trusts the unfamiliar Eastern European start to do the heavy
lifting. Phil Keoghan hosts a spring outing that earns its
slot without reaching for novelty.
