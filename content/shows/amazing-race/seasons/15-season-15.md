Twelve teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg autumn route
through Japan, Vietnam, Cambodia, Dubai, Sweden, Estonia, and
the Netherlands. CBS held the Sunday hour and the production
widened its cast count by one — a small structural tweak that
the editing room used to open the first two legs into broader
team-by-team coverage. The casting goes deep on archetypes the
audience already trusts. Phil Keoghan paces a fall outing
whose geography is sharper than the years on either side.
