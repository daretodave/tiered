Eleven teams, a route marker, and a host nobody had heard of
yet. The format invented itself on the road across five
continents, and CBS aired it on a thin budget through the
fall of 2001. The casting leans on real pairings rather than
archetypes, and the editing trusts the geography to carry the
hour. Phil Keoghan finds the cadence almost immediately.
Every race season that follows is built from the bones laid
down here.
