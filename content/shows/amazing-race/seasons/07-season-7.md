The crossover season, with a pair of returning Survivor
players folded into the lineup and CBS leaning into the
publicity. Ratings climbed sharply across the spring, and
the route ran through South America, southern Africa and
back across the Pacific. The casting around the headliners
runs deep on regional pairings the audience already
recognized. Phil Keoghan works the pit stops with sharper
edges. This is the season that moved the show from cult
favorite to network anchor.
