Eleven teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg autumn route
through China, Russia, Turkey, Spain, the Netherlands,
France, Bangladesh, and Indonesia before a New York finish.
CBS held the Sunday hour and the production ran a workmanlike
fall outing in the franchise's twelfth year on the road. The
casting widens across partner types again. Phil Keoghan hosts
the format running its trusted lane — the season is competent
without making a distinctive argument about itself.
