Season eleven runs the Lopez–Tyler–Jackson panel through its second and final year. The audition circuit found strong coverage across cities, and the panel had settled into a working dynamic — more encouraging than the Cowell era, with Tyler's rock sensibility keeping the critique from drifting toward pure warmth. Neither Jennifer Lopez nor Steven Tyler returned after this season. What the panel built across two seasons ended here, and the format would reset again in season twelve.
