Season eight closes out the original panel's unbroken run with what many consider its strongest audition class. The Hollywood round brought through a top twelve with genuine range — gospel-rooted singers alongside theatrical pop performers — and the competition ran with that variety across the studio weeks. Simon Cowell, Randy Jackson, and Paula Abdul were working together for the last time without knowing the outcome. That gives the season a retrospective weight the viewing didn't carry in real time.
