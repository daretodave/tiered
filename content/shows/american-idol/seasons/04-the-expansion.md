Season four extends the format in nearly every direction — more audition cities, a longer studio run, higher production ambition. The original panel brought the chemistry that made season three work, and there are stretches where the competition runs at a high level. What keeps it fifth in the founding canon is a field that didn't sustain the depth of the seasons above it. The expansion showed what Idol could scale to; the field didn't match it.
