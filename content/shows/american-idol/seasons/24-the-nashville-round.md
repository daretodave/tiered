Season twenty-four tore up the format's oldest piece of furniture — the
multi-city audition tour — for a single stop at Belmont University in
Nashville. Hollywood Week survived the move, rebranded Music City
Takeover; a new 'Ohana Round filmed at Aulani in Hawaii, and the voting
app was retired for website, social, and text voting. Luke Bryan, Lionel
Richie, and Carrie Underwood returned with a season's chemistry already
built, and the premiere drew Idol's biggest audience in four years.
