Season ten arrives as a cultural reset, and the panel is the story. Jennifer Lopez returning to weekly prime time was a major television moment on its own. Steven Tyler brought a rock aesthetic that no previous Idol judge had held. Randy Jackson remained as continuity. The pivot toward encouragement over blunt critique changed how the audition rounds played — warmer, less adversarial, different rather than worse. A format document worth watching for the panel chemistry alone.
