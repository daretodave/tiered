Season twelve features the most turbulent judging panel in American Idol's run. Nicki Minaj and Mariah Carey's on-camera friction was covered extensively in entertainment press before the season aired and continued throughout. Keith Urban was present as a working judge; Randy Jackson shifted to a mentor role mid-season. The contestant competition ran in parallel with the panel drama, but the drama is what viewers remember. Ratings fell. The season documents what happens when the panel becomes the show.
