Season six brought ten fresh survivalists to the Mongolian landscape that the Redemption cast had introduced a year earlier. Without the returning-contestant framing, the format ran on the biome alone — and northern Mongolia delivered. The steppe and taiga combination produces survival conditions markedly different from anything North American, and a new cast encountered it without prior reference. The season closes the no-host era on familiar terrain made fresh by ten first-timers facing it without a road map.
