Season five introduced two things simultaneously: a returning-contestant format and a location the show had never visited. Northern Mongolia's steppe and taiga gave ten survivalists from the first four seasons terrain colder and more arid than anything the franchise had used before. The returning format gave the audience a reason to care about gear choices in a more personal way — viewers knew these survivalists and their previous limits. The Redemption subtitle earns its weight.
