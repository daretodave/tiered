Season seven runs the Power Apron structure at a settled pace, with Shannon Bennett replacing Kylie Kwong as the recurring Immunity mentor. Marco Pierre White returns for a second dedicated week — an unusual distinction that reflects the production's confidence in the format he'd established the prior year. Massimo Bottura guest-appears. The season has no defining structural innovation but holds the founding era's standard with a capable cast and a judging panel still at full authority.
