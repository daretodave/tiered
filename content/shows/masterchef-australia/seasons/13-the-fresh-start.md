Season thirteen is the new panel's first test with a completely fresh field — no returning alumni, no shared history with the judges. Andy Allen, Melissa Leong, and Jock Zonfrillo are confident individually but still calibrating how they read a cast that doesn't know them. A Second Chance Week and a limited Immunity Pin structure keep the competitive stakes visible. Filming extends to Uluru and the Victorian countryside. A solid season that sits below the new panel's best work.
