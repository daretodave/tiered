Season eleven lost two cast members whose social specificity had defined what came before, and both replacements — each only around for one or two seasons — brought more domestically focused energy than RHOA typically runs on. The season is softer in register, leaning into personal-life arcs more than social friction. Tokyo is a genuine highlight. The production made it work, but work was required. A functional season that sits where the ranking puts it.
