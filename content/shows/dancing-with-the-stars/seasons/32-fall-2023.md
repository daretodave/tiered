Season thirty-two introduced Julianne Hough as co-host alongside Alfonso Ribeiro. Hough's background as a professional dancer and former DWTS champion gave the hosting desk a different kind of authority — someone who had stood on the competition floor and knew what the format demanded. The season also simulcast on Disney+ and ABC, returning the show to broadcast. Twelve competitive episodes gave the new hosting team a full run to settle into the rhythm.
