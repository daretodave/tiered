Season twenty-one built a celebrity cast that covered a wider range of ages, backgrounds, and public profiles than most fall editions. That diversity translated into a competitive field with genuine unpredictability — the live-vote dynamic shifted week to week in ways that kept the elimination stakes alive. Twelve episodes gave each partnership room to develop, and the back-half choreography reflected a cast that had committed to the process. Among the single-abc-era fall seasons, it holds the strongest claim.
