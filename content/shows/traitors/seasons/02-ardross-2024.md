Season two is the breakout. The US version stopped trailing the UK original and found its own identity: the civilian mix gives way to an all-reality-personality cast, the release model shifts to a three-episode drop then weekly, and a companion after-show extends the run. Protection moves from the season-one Armory into the challenges. The casting energy is sharp and the hour simply runs better. This is the season the show's reputation now rests on.
