The franchise's first attempt at staged returnees-arrival
storytelling. MTV ran a newer cast through the standard
team-versus-team format for the first stretch, then delivered
a wave of Challenge champions mid-season to take over. The
structural twist is the cast arrival itself; the format is
built around the pivot. The architectural idea — staged
returnees arrival — proved durable enough that the franchise
leaned on the shape in later seasons. The canon places it near
the floor of the modern era.
