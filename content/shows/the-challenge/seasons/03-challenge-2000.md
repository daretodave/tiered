The second crossover, and the one that proved the first was not
a fluke. Real World versus Road Rules, six on six, with the
cast now aware that the camera rewards confrontation and
playing accordingly. The missions still feel like leftover game
show ideas, but the team meetings and the in-house politicking
sharpen considerably. The season MTV used to confirm the
format had legs.
