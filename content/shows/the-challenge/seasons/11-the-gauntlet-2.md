season with TJ Lavin at the helm. MTV reran the Veterans versus
Rookies split, kept the elimination round intact, and let a
deeper cast prove the format had legs. Lavin's calmer, more
athletic register replaced Jonny Moseley's earlier rhythm and
stuck. The Challenge of the next decade starts looking like
this one.
