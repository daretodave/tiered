The franchise's first vet-and-rookie pairing experiment. Each
Challenge veteran picked a real-world unknown — a sprinter, a
wrestler, a stranger from outside the cast pool — and competed
paired. The structural idea is simple and durable: import fresh
athleticism without breaking the cast continuity the franchise
had been building since the first crossover. The pairings
produce uneven matches, but the format question is what the
season is really asking.
