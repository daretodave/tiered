The cleanest format the franchise had attempted to date. Men on
one side, women on the other, two captains, and a structural
clarity the earlier seasons did not have. Jonny Moseley steps
in as the new host and brings a calmer, more athletic energy to
the proceedings than the show had been used to. The casts are
sprawling, the missions are bigger, and the gender-split
template becomes a franchise staple from here forward.
