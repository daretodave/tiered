The franchise's first three-team experiment. MTV split the cast
into Red, Blue, and Gray tribes for the structural variety,
then ran the elimination round through the team-loss math the
divide unlocked. The three-way grammar produced angles the
two-team split never could — temporary alliances, third-place
math, missions that rewarded calculated middle finishes. A
late-era format pivot the franchise carried forward in shape,
if not in name.
