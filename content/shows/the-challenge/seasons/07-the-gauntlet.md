The format-defining season. MTV introduced The Gauntlet — a
one-on-one head-to-head between a losing team's pick and a
challenger from the winning side — and the franchise stopped
being a points race. Suddenly every loss had a name attached.
Jonny Moseley is back, the team split puts veterans against
rookies, and the show now has the structural beat it had been
missing. The franchise's modern grammar starts here.
