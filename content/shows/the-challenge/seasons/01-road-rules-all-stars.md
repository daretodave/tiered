MTV's first attempt to spin Road Rules into something more
competitive. Seven veterans from the first three Road Rules
casts climbed back into an RV and chased missions across the
American West, with a charity prize on the line. The pacing is
loose, the format barely exists yet, and the show is still
finding what makes returnees compelling on camera. Watch it as
the rough draft of every season that follows.
