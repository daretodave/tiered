The season that introduced the season-versus-season structure
the franchise still uses. Rather than one Real World team and
one Road Rules team, MTV grouped contestants by their original
season — turning old roommates into teammates and old cast
rivalries into team rivalries. The pacing is uneven and the
team count is large, but the structural idea here would echo
through every Battle of the Seasons that followed.
