The season where the franchise stopped pretending the missions
were the side dish. MTV invested in bigger stunts, taller drops,
longer endurance work, and rebuilt the show around physical
spectacle. The Real World versus Road Rules split remains, but
the editing now spends real time on the challenges themselves.
A pivot point. After this the franchise stops being a road
trip with games and starts being a competition with a cast.
