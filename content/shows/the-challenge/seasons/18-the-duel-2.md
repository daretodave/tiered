The Duel returns as the franchise's second individual-format
season. No teams, no pairs — every player ran solo, and a loss
meant a head-to-head Duel. The repeat proved the format
question the original Duel asked: a Challenge season could
stand without team grammar. The location widened the franchise's
visual register, and the season tilted toward veterans who had
already proven they could carry an individual arc.
