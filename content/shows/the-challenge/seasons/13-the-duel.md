The franchise's first individual format. No teams, no pairs —
every player ran on their own and faced a head-to-head Duel
when they lost. The structural pivot mattered: a Challenge
season could now stand on personal arc rather than team grammar,
and the format would return as a sequel and as a feature of
later runs. The cast leaned veteran. Mexico stood in for the
desert.
