The franchise's second vet-and-rookie pairing experiment, with
the same structural idea as Fresh Meat I — Challenge veterans
choosing fresh athletic talent from outside the cast pool. The
pairings sharpen because the format is now familiar, but the
season's argument is still the same as its predecessor: how
much of The Challenge is the cast continuity, and how much is
replaceable. The hour does not quite settle the question.
