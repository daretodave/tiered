The franchise's Survivor experiment. MTV stripped the format
down to bare beach, scattered supplies, and a daily challenge
for resources — the elimination round folded into the survival
premise itself. The departure from the team-versus-team spine
is real, and the result is the season most divergent from the
franchise's grammar. The Island sits as a one-off — the show
that Challenge wasn't going to be, demonstrated and set aside.
