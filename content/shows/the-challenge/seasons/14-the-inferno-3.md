Good Guys versus Bad Asses for the third time, with the format
showing its first signs of fatigue. MTV ran the archetype split
again, the elimination room kept its Inferno branding, and the
cast tilted toward franchise regulars. The hour is competent
and the missions are scaled up, but the structural surprise of
the first two Infernos is gone. The season reads as a franchise
consolidating rather than discovering.
