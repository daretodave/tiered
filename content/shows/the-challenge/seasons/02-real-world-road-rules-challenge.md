The season that named the show. MTV took six Real World alumni
and six Road Rules alumni, split them into rival teams, and let
the cultural difference between the two casts carry the drama.
Real World kids were sharper-tongued; Road Rules kids were
fitter and more competitive. The format is still rough and the
missions are scrappy, but the team-vs-team spine that defines
the franchise locks into place here.
