# Editor's Canon — The Real Housewives of Miami

## 1. Season 1

tag: The founding season that launched Miami's social world — before the cancellation, before the revival.
slot_argument: Six women enter Miami's social scene in a debut confident enough to survive what came next — a three-season run, a cancellation, and an eventual return across two networks. The cast set a tone the franchise would keep circling back to.
community_rank_hint: rank=1 delta=0 sentiment=hold

Season one earns its spot because it's the only version of RHOM that didn't yet know what the franchise would become. Marysol Patton, Alexia Echevarria, Adriana de Moura, Cristy Rice, Larsa Pippen, and Lea Black introduce a Miami social world built on glamour, family businesses, and old friendships tested by new money — sunnier and more overtly aspirational than most Housewives debuts. Nothing here is hedged against a future cancellation or a Peacock relaunch; the cast plays it straight, and that unguarded quality is hard to replicate once a show knows its own reputation. Every season since has had to answer to this one's confidence.
