Boston brings in Dr. Jessica Griffin, a local psychologist, to replace Rachel DeAlto — the panel's second shakeup in as many years. Schwartz and Roberson return, and the show sets three new couples down in Griffin's home city for sixteen episodes. By Season 6 the format has a rhythm: new city, mostly familiar experts, the same legal-marriage premise running with none of the uncertainty that once made it a risk.
