Charlotte brings in Dr. Viviana Coles to replace Dr. Jessica Griffin, the third time in the show's run that one expert seat has changed hands. Schwartz and Roberson return, matching three new couples in Charlotte across fifteen episodes. By now the swap itself is a familiar beat — a new specialist steps in, two familiar faces stay, and the blind-marriage format runs exactly as it has since Boston.
