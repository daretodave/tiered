New Orleans keeps the five-couple format and the Schwartz, Roberson, and Coles panel from Washington, but the season's broadcast runs far longer than usual — closer to four months than the typical two-month arc, as production adjusts to pandemic conditions. Five new couples marry across a megasized run of roughly seventeen episodes. The format holds steady; it's the pace around it that's unlike anything the show had done before.
