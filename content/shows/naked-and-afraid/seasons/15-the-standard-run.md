Season fifteen delivered the modern era's casting approach and production priorities without the novelty weight of an opening season. Season fourteen had to establish what the modern era was; fifteen had to prove the approach worked a second time. The 21-day structure, the remote locations, the pairing dynamic — all running from the format's current production assumptions. A settled season earns a different kind of credit than a transitional one, and this season earns it.
