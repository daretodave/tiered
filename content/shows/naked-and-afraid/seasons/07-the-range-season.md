Season seven's contribution was geographic range. The format made a broader case that the 21-day premise held across meaningfully different environments — varied biomes, different protein sources, different shelter demands — rather than returning to proven terrain. Each pairing faced a distinct environmental argument, which kept the season from settling into the repetitive rhythm that a format this structurally simple can fall into. The founding era's case for the show's versatility, delivered a season before the run closed.
