The founding era's closing season arrived with the format fully stable. The PSR scoring system was established language, the production rhythm was assured, and the paired-survival premise had been tested across enough varied environments to confirm its durability. Season eight didn't need to break new ground — it needed to close a long opening chapter cleanly. It did. The locations were demanding, the pairings were credibly matched, and the 21-day structure delivered without leaning on novelty.
