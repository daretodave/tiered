The six-shark bench that season five introduced holds without a
single roster change — no arrivals, no departures, just the same
six trading the rotation across 29 episodes. Episode eight is
billed as a Veterans' Small Business Week special, and episode
nine marks the show's 100th episode with a retrospective look
back. GoPro founder Nick Woodman guests twice. The finale leads
straight into the premiere of a new companion series, Beyond the
Tank.
