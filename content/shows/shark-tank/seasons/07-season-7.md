Season seven keeps the classic six fully intact, but adds a new
wrinkle: guest sharks start rotating through an added seat rather
than filling in for an absence. It's the first sign the panel
might grow past six without displacing anyone already there.
Twenty-nine episodes settle into a steady rhythm, the tank's
familiar six carrying most pitches while a rotating guest chair
tests whether a bigger bench works.
