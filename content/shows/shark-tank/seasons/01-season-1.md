Shark Tank's first season is lean and untested — seven episodes, a
five-shark panel of Kevin Harrington, Barbara Corcoran, Kevin
O'Leary, Daymond John, and Robert Herjavec, and entrepreneurs
pitching without knowing yet that this show would become
appointment television. The tank set is smaller, the questioning
less refined, but the premise — real money, real stakes, minutes
to make the case — lands fully formed from the first pitch.
