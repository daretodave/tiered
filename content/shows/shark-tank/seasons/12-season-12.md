Season twelve trades Culver City for a Las Vegas soundstage,
filming the entire run inside a strict pandemic bubble. The
classic six sit six feet apart, waves and fist bumps standing in
for handshakes, and the set itself looks nothing like any season
before it. Twenty-five episodes prove the pitch format survives
even under those constraints — the tension holds regardless of
the distance.
