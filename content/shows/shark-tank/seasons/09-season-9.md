Season nine is the one real stress test the classic six's
stability ever faced: one of the six sits out a stretch of the
season, and guest sharks fill the gap more heavily than at any
point since the lineup came together. It's the steadiest years
this canon has rewarded meeting their first real interruption,
and while the remaining five hold the panel's chemistry together
well enough, this reads as the least settled the classic-six era
ever gets.
