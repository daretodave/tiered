Season seven is the sequel that hit even bigger. Ariana Madix returns for a second run in Fiji, and the season posts the franchise's biggest viewership yet, with hundreds of millions of video views generated across social media during its run. The recurring Casa Amor twist returns in week three, and thirty islanders cycle through the villa across six weeks. The season also owns an early, public casting stumble: two islanders exit the villa days apart after old posts resurface.
