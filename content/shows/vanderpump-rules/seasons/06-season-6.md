Season 6 is the show's biggest expansion so far. Brittany Cartwright and Lala Kent move up to full series regulars, and Billie Lee and Raquel Leviss join in recurring roles, growing the ensemble to its largest size yet. Twenty-five episodes, the longest order the show has run, give that bigger cast real space to work through SUR's hierarchy without anyone getting lost in the crowd. Nobody leaves. The format just keeps scaling.
