Jerry Springer replaces Regis Philbin as host and brings a comfortable ease with chaotic live television to the stage. Piers Morgan, Sharon Osbourne, and David Hasselhoff return as a panel that has developed real working chemistry from the first season. The audition pool covers familiar ground with greater confidence, and the production understands the format's rhythm better than it did in 2006. A more controlled run than the debut — more assured, less electric.
