A new cast member integrates well, and the season's international legs give RHOBH some of its most visually distinctive material. Hong Kong and Dubai are more demanding backdrops than a return trip to a European spa town — the cast has to operate without the familiar social infrastructure of home, and the pressure reads on screen. The cast combination feels settled here in ways the immediately preceding seasons didn't quite manage. Solid established-era television.
