The season that started everything. RHOBH arrives with a setting that does more narrative work than the standard Housewives launch — Beverly Hills has a specific social architecture, a particular way class and performance intersect, and the debut cast navigates it with the confidence of people who've lived inside it. The format isn't fully fluent yet, but the casting is. The foundational document: everything that follows builds on what this season establishes.
