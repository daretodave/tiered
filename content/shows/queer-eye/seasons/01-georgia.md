Eight episodes, one Atlanta-area nominee each week, and five specialists working in parallel — food and wine, fashion, grooming, culture, and design. The reboot keeps the original's makeover bones but spends more time on conversation than transformation, and the Fab Five's chemistry is immediate. Georgia gives the season a specific, unglamorous backdrop that grounds the whole format before the show ever leaves the state.
