The format that would become a Bravo staple assembled itself here, aboard a superyacht off Sint Maarten. Captain Lee Rosbach commanded his first crew through a charter season built on service pressure, close-quarters tension, and the peculiar social experiment of living where you work. The cast brought confrontational energy and the clear-water Caribbean backdrop gave the show a visual register it would return to again. Rough at the edges, essential to everything that followed.
