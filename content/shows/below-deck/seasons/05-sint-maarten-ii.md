Below Deck returned to Sint Maarten and demonstrated that cast turnover was part of the format's design, not a liability. A new crew, the same superyacht structure, the same Caribbean waters — and a noticeably sharper hierarchy tension in the interior department. Captain Lee navigated the boat and the personalities with his characteristic directness. The second Sint Maarten run proved the format had found its identity and could sustain it through repeated location visits.
