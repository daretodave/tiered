Dallas is the low point of the founding run. The format holds and the production stays competent, but the pod sequences — where the show either earns its premise or exposes it — never generate the depth the other founding-batch cities manage. The cast's engagement feels shallower here than elsewhere, and that gap is visible throughout. The weakest of the five, but worth a watch for the full franchise arc.
