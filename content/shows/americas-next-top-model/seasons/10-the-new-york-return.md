New York City as the production base changes what the show looks like. The street-casting
energy, the fashion industry proximity, and the density of editorial-ready locations give
the photoshoots a register the LA cycles rarely matched. Paulina Porizkova brings sharper
runway and commercial expertise to the panel, and the expanded 14-contestant pool gives
the competition more room to breathe. Rome closes the season with strong European
editorial work. A structurally significant cycle that earns its rank.
