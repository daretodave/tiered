Cycle 20 makes the largest structural change in the show's CW run: opening the
competition to male contestants for the first time. The co-ed cast changes the
photoshoot dynamics and the panel criteria in ways that are genuinely interesting —
pairs work, mixed-gender editorial contexts, and a competition that can't rely on
the conventions built across nineteen prior cycles. Brazil delivers strong location
material across both Rio and São Paulo. The boldest format move of the post-C15
CW era.
