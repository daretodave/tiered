The one that started it, and it shows. Thirteen contestants, a UPN budget, and a host
building the format in real time — the makeover, the elimination panel, the photoshoot
as competition metric. Tyra Banks arrived with a clear concept and constructed it on
camera, rough edges and all. The casting pool is small and unguarded in ways no later
cycle can replicate. The grammar every later season inherited, drafted here at lower
resolution.
