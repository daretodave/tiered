the only South American stop the show ever made, and the production treats it as a
genuine editorial opportunity rather than a backdrop. The casting carries real range,
giving the panel sessions competitive substance. Paulina Porizkova closes her run here
with the same fashion precision she brought to the panel from cycle ten. A reliable,
well-structured season without a weak leg.
