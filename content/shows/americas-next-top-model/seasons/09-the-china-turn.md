The China Turn is the most ambitious location package of the CW's early cycles — Shanghai
and Beijing each contribute distinct visual registers, and the photoshoots use both cities
with real craft. Twiggy closes her run on the permanent panel here, and her presence keeps
the judging sessions grounded. The season's weakness is the competition layer itself, which
runs unevenly and produces a polarizing result. The travel is strong; the competitive
tension is not.
