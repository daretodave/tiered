Cycle 19's casting restriction — college students only — cuts in both directions.
The contestants arrive with less industry conditioning, which occasionally produces
candid competitive dynamics, but the restriction narrows the casting range the show
consistently relied on. Oxford and Crete give the international legs real editorial
texture. A competent middle placement in the late CW era — not the experiment C18 was,
not as forgettable as what followed.
