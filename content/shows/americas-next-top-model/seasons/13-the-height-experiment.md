Cycle 13 is the CW era's clearest format miscalculation. The explicit height cap narrows
the contestant pool in ways that limit competitive range rather than deepen it. Staying
entirely domestic removes the format's strongest creative engine, and the reduction to
three judges strips the critique sessions of texture. The domestic locations — LA, Las
Vegas, Maui — are visually attractive but not editorial in the way the international
legs consistently were.
