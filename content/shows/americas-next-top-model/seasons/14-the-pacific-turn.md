The Pacific Turn delivers what cycle 13 withheld — a strong international location and
a reinvigorated panel. New Zealand's varied terrain produces photoshoots that range from
urban editorial to landscape-scale drama, including a distinctive shoot at the Hobbiton
film set. André Leon Talley brings Vogue authority to the judging table that the panel
had lacked since Paulina Porizkova's departure. A reliable cycle that earns its place
without distinguishing itself — the international return matters more than the competition.
