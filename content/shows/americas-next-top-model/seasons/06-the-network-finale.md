The last UPN cycle closes with real ambition. Bangkok and Phuket mark the show's first
Southeast Asian shoot, and the photoshoot creative uses the location — temples, markets,
and coastal light the earlier cycles never had. Twiggy joins the permanent panel for her
first full cycle, sharpening the fashion editorial register. The competition runs cleanly
on a format the UPN years had fully settled. A strong final chapter for the network that
launched the show.
