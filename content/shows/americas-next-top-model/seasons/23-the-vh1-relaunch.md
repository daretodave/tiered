Cycle 23 is the weakest season the show produced. The network move to VH1 brings a
new host in Rita Ora and a format retool that makes social-media following a competitive
criterion alongside photoshoot performance — a structural choice that dilutes what the
competition was built to measure. The 10-episode run leaves little room to develop, and
staying in Los Angeles removes the location work the format relied on. The relaunch is
the show at its lowest ebb.
