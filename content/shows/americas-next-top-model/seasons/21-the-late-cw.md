Cycle 21 is the most straightforwardly unremarkable season of the late CW run.
The format reverts to open casting after C18 and C20's experiments, and staying
entirely in Los Angeles removes the location engine that gave the better cycles their
editorial ambition. The casting is solid, the photoshoots functional — but there is
nothing here the earlier domestic stretches hadn't already covered more memorably.
