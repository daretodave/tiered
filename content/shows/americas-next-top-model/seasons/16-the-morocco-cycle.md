Cycle 16 is a reliable CW-era entry that earns its place without distinguishing itself.
Marrakech gives the international leg a genuinely different visual context — the medina
architecture, textiles, and desert-edge landscape produce photoshoots with real
editorial texture. The competition back in Los Angeles runs competently without
generating the tension the better cycles sustain. A solid middle-of-the-road season
that shows the format working at cruise altitude.
