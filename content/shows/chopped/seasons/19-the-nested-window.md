Thirteen episodes run from February into June 2014, sitting almost entirely inside Season 18's own air window — only its final few weeks spill past. It's the deepest single-season overlap the format has aired to this point, though nothing about an individual episode changes: the locked basket, three-round structure, and Ted Allen's hosting all run exactly as designed. The tangle lives entirely in the release calendar.
