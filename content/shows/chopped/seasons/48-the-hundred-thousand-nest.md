Thirteen episodes run from December 2020 into March 2021: one standalone hour, then a five-part Grudge Match tournament building to a $100,000 finale, a four-part Meat Fight bracket, and three Hangry Baskets episodes (a fourth airs the following season). The entire run sits inside both Season 47's and Season 49's overlapping windows at once — a doubly nested calendar this canon hasn't measured before, offset by the densest tournament stretch it has run.
