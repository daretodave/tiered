Season thirteen is RHOC's most transitional — two new cast members, a reduced role for a franchise original, and the clearest signal yet that the show is rebuilding. Gina Kirschenheiter and Emily Simpson arrive with genuine chemistry between themselves, but their integration with the existing cast is uneven across the run. The transition era is underway in earnest, and the season's variable energy reflects that construction work.
