Season sixteen is the revival era's clearest argument. Three cast members exit, Heather Dubrow returns after four seasons away, and Noella Bergener and Dr. Jen Armstrong arrive with chemistry that clicks faster than most new additions manage. Heather's return reshapes the social register immediately — her axis of tension with Shannon Beador is renewed and layered with more history than their first run together. The cast feels built rather than assembled. The OC social architecture has genuine purpose again.
