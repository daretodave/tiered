Season six marks the start of RHOC's peak era with a cast reconfiguration that shifts the show's social world upward. Heather Dubrow's arrival changes the economic register of the group and gives the dynamics a new axis of tension — aspiration, taste, and competing versions of what it means to do well in Orange County. The production is fluent, the cast chemistry is immediate, and the format uses the new social geometry with purpose.
