Head Chefs Only makes the strongest casting argument the themed era has produced outside the All-Stars edition. Every contestant was already running a kitchen — no line cooks, no students, no chefs still building toward their first leadership role. The move to Foxwoods in Connecticut marked the franchise's first East Coast production. Michelle Tribble and James Avery take the sous-chef positions. The talent floor shifts upward immediately.
