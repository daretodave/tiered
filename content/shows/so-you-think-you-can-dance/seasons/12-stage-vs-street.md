Season twelve breaks from a decade of men-versus-women teams, splitting
finalists into Stage and Street squads instead — mentored by Travis Wall
and Stephen 'tWitch' Boss. The five-city audition tour hits New York,
Dallas, Detroit, Memphis, and Los Angeles, and a special mid-season
episode, A Decade of Dance, marks the franchise's tenth anniversary on
the air. Jason Derulo joins the judging panel alongside Nigel Lythgoe
and Paula Abdul, rounding out the season's changes.
