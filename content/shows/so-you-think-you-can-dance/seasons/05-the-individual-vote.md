Season five moves into a new CBS Studios soundstage — its last
season on that stage — and reworks the format's biggest structural
rule: once the field reaches the Top 10, couple-based voting gives
way to individual voting, with partners reshuffled at random every
week. Auditions sweep Brooklyn, Miami, Los Angeles, Denver, Memphis,
and Seattle before Vegas callbacks at Planet Hollywood. The season
closes at the Kodak Theatre ahead of a forty-city tour.
