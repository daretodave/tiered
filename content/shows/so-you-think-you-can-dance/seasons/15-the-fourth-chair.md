Season fifteen expands the judging panel to four, with Stephen 'tWitch'
Boss joining Nigel Lythgoe, Mary Murphy, and Vanessa Hudgens for both
auditions and live shows — her final season on the panel. A mid-season
cut trims the roster before the competition's later rounds, tightening
the field heading into its second half. It's a season built on
structural adjustment rather than reinvention.
