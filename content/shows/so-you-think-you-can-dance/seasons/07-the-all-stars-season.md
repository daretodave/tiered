Season seven reinvents the format around All-Stars — instead of
pairing with each other, the Top 11 finalists draw a rotating pool of
former-contestant specialists each week, one per style. The field
shrinks from the usual twenty to eleven, and Mary Murphy steps back
from the permanent panel to guest appearances only. Performance
broadcasts go live for the first time, and the season airs in HD for
the first time as well.
