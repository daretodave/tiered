Season fourteen course-corrects after a year built entirely around
kids, returning the competition to its adult cast. Ten returning
All-Stars — including Gaby Diaz, Comfort Fedoke, and Allison Holker —
shift from live-show partners to Academy-round judges and mentors,
guiding the Top 10 through auditions. Nigel Lythgoe and Mary Murphy
anchor the panel, joined by newcomer Vanessa Hudgens, in a season built
on stability rather than reinvention.
