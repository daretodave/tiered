Season eighteen relocates production entirely to Atlanta's Pullman
Yards, the first time the show has filmed outside its usual Los
Angeles base. The open-call audition tour gives way to video
submissions, with a Top 100 invited directly to the city, and
auditions film documentary-style on a flat floor with no live
audience. JoJo Siwa joins the panel after a judging change, and
eliminations run on judges' votes alone.
