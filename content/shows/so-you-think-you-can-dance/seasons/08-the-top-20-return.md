Season eight returns to the classic twenty-dancer field after last
season's reduced Top 11, splitting evenly between ten men and ten
women. The All-Stars mechanic carries over but arrives later —
rotating specialists only join once the field narrows to the Top 10,
blending the format's original bracket with the prior season's
innovation. Adam Shankman doesn't return to the permanent panel, and
fans can vote by text and online for the first time.
