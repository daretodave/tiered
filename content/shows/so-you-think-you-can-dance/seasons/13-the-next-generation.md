Season thirteen rebrands the show entirely as The Next Generation,
opening the competition to dancers aged eight to thirteen for the first
time. Ten returning All-Stars each mentor a young finalist through the
live rounds, and Maddie Ziegler joins the panel as a full fourth judge
alongside Nigel Lythgoe, Paula Abdul, and Jason Derulo. The season's
thirteen-episode order is the shortest since the show's debut.
