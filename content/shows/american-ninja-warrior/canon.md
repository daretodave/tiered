# Editor's Canon — American Ninja Warrior

## 1. The Proving Ground

The first season earns the top slot by default, but it earns it honestly too. American Ninja Warrior hadn't figured out yet that it wanted to be its own show — this is still a scouting mission for Sasuke, built around the idea that somewhere in America there's someone who can hang with Japan's best. That premise gives the qualifying rounds in Venice a genuine underdog energy the later, more produced seasons have to work harder to manufacture. The obstacles are simpler and the production is smaller, but the format's whole appeal — watching confident people get humbled by a wall — is already fully formed.
