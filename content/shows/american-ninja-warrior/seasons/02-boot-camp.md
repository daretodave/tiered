Season two keeps the single-city format — everyone still qualifies through Venice Beach — but adds Ninja Warrior Boot Camp, a team-based elimination stage in the California mountains that stands between the qualifiers and the trip to Mount Midoriyama. Matt Iseman steps in as lead host, with Jimmy Smith on commentary and Alison Haislip on the sidelines. The course is still simple by later standards, but the stakes get sharper.
