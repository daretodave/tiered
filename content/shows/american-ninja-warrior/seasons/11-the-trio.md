Season eleven pushes into the Pacific Northwest for the first time, with an indoor qualifying course at the Tacoma Dome joining Los Angeles, Atlanta, Oklahoma City, Baltimore, and Cincinnati. Qualifiers now choose between the Warped Wall and Mega Wall, each with three attempts and stepped bonuses. A new Power Tower obstacle offers a qualifying speed pass and a finals safety pass. Zuri Hall joins the booth, replacing Kristine Leahy.
