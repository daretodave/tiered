Season ten restores the standard four-stage national finals and rotates through six qualifying cities, welcoming Minneapolis for the first time while Dallas, Miami, Indianapolis, and Philadelphia all make their first returns in years. The new Mega Wall — an 18-foot climb with one attempt and a $10,000 bonus — debuts at qualifying. The minimum competitor age drops to 19, and a $100,000 fallback prize rewards the furthest-advancing competitor.
