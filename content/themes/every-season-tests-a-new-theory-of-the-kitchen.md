# Every season tests a new theory of the kitchen

The white apron, the mystery box, and Gordon Ramsay barely move from
year to year. What actually changes is the answer to a simpler
question: who's in the kitchen together, and why. Mentor-drafted
teams, an all-returnee bench, generational cohorts, fixed pairs,
global brackets — MasterChef keeps swapping the organizing idea
under its own format, season after season, long before it settles
on one for good.
