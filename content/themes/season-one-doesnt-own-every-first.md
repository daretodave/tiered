# Season one doesn't own every first

A premiere gets the credit for inventing a format, but a lot of
a show's real firsts land much later, once the cameras already
know what they're doing. A casting milestone, a location no
earlier crew could clear, a rule change nobody needed until
season four or fifteen or eighteen — these are the seasons that
made the update, long after the debut had already had its say.
