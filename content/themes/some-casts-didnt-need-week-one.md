# Some casts didn't need week one

Dancing with the Stars runs on a built-in premise: celebrities who
can't dance yet, learning fast enough to survive the week. Most
seasons deliver exactly that arc. These don't. An Olympic medal, a
Paralympic record, or a professional dancer's own prior training
did some or all of the format's early teaching before the premiere
even aired — and a few seasons on this list prove the opposite is
just as watchable, when nobody gets the shortcut at all.
