**Show-add arming rule.** A new show may be added only when the
gap table below reads zero AND ≥ 14 days have passed since the
last add completed its full drain. A sweep-found gap re-locks
adds until it clears. When an add fires, append a show-add log
row (date filed, slug); when its drain completes, fill the
drain-completed date — the next 14-day window measures from
that.
